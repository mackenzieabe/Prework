Billie Holiday Fan Page

Bio

Billie Holiday was discovered at a club in Harlem, NY when she was eighteen. Lady Day, as she was nicknamed, would go on to become one of the most well known names in jazz. She had a successful career for twenty years but, sadly, it was cut short due to addiction. She passed away in 1959 but her legacy lives on through her music.
